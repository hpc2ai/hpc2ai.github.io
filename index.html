About the Center

The INF-HPC2AI is a premier research center dedicated to advancing scientific and technological frontiers through high-performance computing (HPC), with a strategic emphasis on artificial intelligence (AI). Hosted at the Institute of Informatics – UFRGS, the center integrates interdisciplinary research, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and collaborative innovation.

Research Areas

Our research spans scalable AI algorithms, heterogeneous computing, climate modeling, bioinformatics, natural language processing, and intelligent systems for scientific discovery. HPC infrastructure is leveraged to accelerate and scale these domains, delivering impactful outcomes across science, industry, and society.

Interested in collaborating or learning more? Reach out to our team at hpc2ai@inf.ufrgs.br. We welcome partnerships with academic, industrial, and governmental organizations.
