Our Research

Exascale AI Inference for Scientific Workloads

Development of scalable AI models capable of running on exascale supercomputers for weather, genomics, and astrophysics applications.

Energy-Efficient GPU Scheduling

Dynamic resource allocation and frequency tuning strategies to reduce power usage without compromising HPC performance.

Large-Scale AI Model Optimization

Parallel hyperparameter tuning and pruning for billion-parameter models on distributed multi-GPU environments.

Fault-Tolerant Deep Learning

Techniques for resilience and recovery in deep learning training under hardware or communication failures in HPC clusters.

In-Situ Visualization with AI Assistance

Real-time visualization and pattern recognition using shared GPU execution between simulation and analysis components.

AI-Driven Resource Management in HPC Clouds

Autonomous scheduling and auto-scaling of cloud-native HPC workloads using reinforcement learning and predictive models.
